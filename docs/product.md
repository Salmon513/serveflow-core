# ServeFlow Core

## Vision

ServeFlow Core is an AI-powered restaurant operations platform.

The platform helps restaurants automate:
- customer communication
- WhatsApp workflows
- booking management
- operational workflows
- AI-assisted customer support

The project starts as a focused operational automation MVP and evolves into a scalable SaaS platform over time.

---

# Current Stage

MVP foundation phase.

Current priority:
build a working AI WhatsApp Restaurant Assistant.

---

# MVP V1 Scope

Initial features:

- Menu Q&A
- Booking flow
- FAQ support
- Customer information capture

---

# Target Customers

Primary:
- Restaurants
- Cafes
- Small hospitality businesses

Secondary:
- SMEs with customer workflow automation needs

---

# Founder Priorities

Priority order:

1. Ship quickly
2. Learn customer pain
3. Validate workflows
4. Productize repeated problems
5. Scale intentionally

---

# Product Philosophy

ServeFlow Core should feel:
- operational
- reliable
- fast
- maintainable
- automation-first

Avoid:
- unnecessary feature bloat
- AI gimmicks
- premature enterprise complexity

---

# Long-Term Vision

Evolve into:
- multi-tenant SaaS
- workflow automation platform
- restaurant operations OS
- analytics platform
- customer retention system

But ONLY after customer validation.
